Weekly Sales

If you want to keep up with our weekly sales that we put out, check out our Facebook and Instagram. Joe, the butcher and part-time owner, is big on social media and uses it a lot.

You can find a lot of info on coupons, weekly sales, updates/news about the store, and lots more, on Joe's social media. He is very active, and will always provide the most up-to-date information.

Do not fall for any scams. Joe will not ask for your personal information on ANY social media platforms.
