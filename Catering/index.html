Catering

We are partnered up with DoorDash, Uber Eats, Postmates, and Grub Hub, to help with catering services. They offer the ability to do group orders, which allows multiple people to add their individual items into a collective order cart at the same time. They also allow for individual orders.

We do not offer any delivery/catering services of our own; only through third-party means.
